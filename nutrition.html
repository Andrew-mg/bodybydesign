NUTRITION

Good protein sources include lean meats like chicken breast, legumes, and fish, which help build and repair tissues while supporting muscle health. Healthy carbohydrates can be found in whole grains, fruits, and vegetables, providing essential energy and fiber for digestion. Foods like quinoa, brown rice, and sweet potatoes are excellent examples of nutritious carbs. Good fats, such as those from avocados, nuts, and olive oil, support brain health, hormone production, and nutrient absorption. Including sources of omega-3 fatty acids, like salmon and flaxseeds, can further enhance heart health. Incorporating a balance of these nutrients promotes overall wellness, sustained energy levels, and optimal bodily functions.
